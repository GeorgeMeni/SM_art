# SM_art

SM_art is the vibrant online presence of 12-year-old South African artist Sophia Menikou. Celebrated for bold, whimsical paintings and award-winning talent, SM_art transforms her art into calendars, notebooks, and gifts while building a joyful, family-run creative business.

---

## 🎨 Project Overview
SM_art is more than an art portfolio — it’s a growing ecosystem that showcases Sophia’s creativity while teaching real-world business skills. The project combines:
- A professional website and online shop
- Social media engagement
- Product development (calendars, notebooks, greeting cards, etc.)
- Branding and storytelling

## 🌟 Mission
To inspire joy through art, nurture young talent, and build a sustainable, family-driven creative brand.

## 📂 Repository Structure
- `/website` → Website code, layouts, and assets  
- `/content` → Artwork images, product descriptions, and marketing copy  
- `/docs` → Guides, notes, and project documentation  
- `/brand` → Logos, color palettes, and style guidelines

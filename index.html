The Artist Behind SM_art

Where talent
meets heart.

Sophia picked up a brush at age 7 and painted a peacock that stopped everyone in the room. Five years later, she's an Ambassadors Award winner at the National Eisteddfod — South Africa's most prestigious arts platform.

Her work is bold, joyful, and unmistakably hers. Wildlife that feels alive. Portraits full of personality. Botanicals that glow. Every piece is hand-painted with joy and integrity — values Sophia lives by.

A portion of every sale goes to animal welfare. Because the animals she paints deserve that.

Sophia's Story

A brush, a blank
canvas, and everything

It started with a peacock. Sophia was seven years old when she painted her first piece — bold, colourful, fearless. Nobody told her how hard painting was supposed to be. She just painted.

Five years on, she paints wildlife that roars off the canvas, portraits full of personality, and watercolours that glow with quiet joy. She has earned four diplomas and the prestigious Ambassadors Award at the National Eisteddfod — recognition that her talent is real, and growing.

SM_art isn't just a name. It's the story of a young artist who paints because she loves it, sells because people love what she creates, and gives a portion of every sale to animal welfare — because the animals she paints deserve that.
